Welcome to pgpNow

With pgpNow you have secure PGP-based encryption right in your browser. Start off by generating a PGP Key or import an existing one

To get started just enter your name so others know who you are (it will be stored in the public key so others can verify the messages)

Note: Generating your keys may take some seconds. Your browser may hang during that time.
